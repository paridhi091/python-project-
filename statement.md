**Problem Statement -**

The project's goal is to develop a straightforward unit converter that enables users to convert values between various mass and length units. This tool offers fast and accurate conversions in an easy-to-use manner because many users have trouble remembering conversion formulas or performing manual calculations.

**Scope of the Project-**

-Converts only length and mass units.

-Basic units like kilometers, meters, centimeters, millimeters, kilograms, grams, and milligrams are supported.

-operates via a straightforward Python program with a menu.

-Focuses on accuracy, simplicity, and ease of use for beginners.

-Has the potential to grow in the future to include more  unit categories.

**Target Users-**

-Students learning basic unit conversions

-Beginners learning Python programming

-Anyone who needs to quickly convert between mass and length units

-Teachers seeking basic demonstration tools

**High-Level Features-**

-Easy navigation with a menu-based interface

-Conversion of length (km → m → cm → mm)

-Conversion of mass (kg → g → mg)

-Both decimal and integer values are accepted.

-Displays accurate and clear results

-Handles invalid inputs gracefully
